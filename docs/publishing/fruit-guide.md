# RiserJuices 1.7.0 Fruit Guide

RiserJuices 1.7.0 adds a survival-ready fruit system. Fruits are no longer creative-only items: they can be found naturally in fitting biomes, harvested, replanted, and used to make drinks with gameplay effects.

## How Fruits Work

Fruit sources generate naturally in the world according to their real shape: tree fruits grow on trees, grapes grow on vines, strawberries and blueberries grow on bushes, and pineapples grow as low tropical plants. Players can collect fruit from these natural sources, obtain saplings or plant blocks, replant them, and build renewable fruit farms.

## Where To Find Each Fruit

- **Lemons**: found on natural lemon trees in forests, jungles, plains, sunflower plains, and meadows.
- **Grapes**: found as wild grape vines in forests, plains, sunflower plains, and meadows.
- **Oranges**: found on orange trees in forests, savannas, plains, sunflower plains, and meadows.
- **Green Apples**: found on green apple trees in forests, plains, sunflower plains, and meadows.
- **Coconuts**: found on coconut palms on beaches and in deserts.
- **Pineapples**: found as tropical pineapple plants in jungles, beaches, and savannas.
- **Strawberries**: found as strawberry bushes in plains, sunflower plains, meadows, and forests.
- **Blueberries**: found as blueberry bushes in taigas and forests.
- **Bananas**: found on banana trees in jungles.
- **Cherries**: found on fruiting cherry trees in cherry groves, flower forests, and meadows.
- **Mangoes**: found on mango trees in jungles and savannas.

## Drink Effects

- **Lemonade**: grants Haste.
- **Grape Juice**: grants short Regeneration.
- **Wine**: grants brief Strength. Drinking more wine while already empowered can cause Nausea.
- **Orange Juice**: grants short Fire Resistance.
- **Green Apple Cider**: grants Speed.
- **Coconut Water**: grants Saturation.
- **Tropical Juice**: grants Speed with a small Hunger effect as balance.
- **Strawberry Smoothie**: grants very short Regeneration.
- **Blueberry Smoothie**: grants Night Vision.
- **Banana Smoothie**: grants Haste and Saturation.
- **Cherry Juice**: grants Luck.
- **Mango Juice**: grants Absorption.

## Future Plans

Future updates will expand the system with larger fruit trees, more natural plant shapes, additional fruit types, cooking stations, deeper farming progression, and more recipes built around juices and fruit-based foods.
